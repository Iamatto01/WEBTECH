About Us

Authentic halal cuisine crafted with love and tradition

Since 2017

Founded in 2017, Rezki Food has been serving authentic halal cuisine to the UIAM community and beyond. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide delicious, wholesome meals prepared with the finest ingredients and traditional recipes.

Every dish we serve tells a story of our heritage and passion for culinary excellence. Our chefs combine traditional cooking methods with modern techniques to create flavors that delight our customers.
